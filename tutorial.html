I'll be producing a step by step Gimp tutorial to help you along in your xraying needs.

Key points to remember are:

  • Big size is win
  • Good lighting is win (no under-exposed or over-exposed pics)
  • Light clothing works best
  • Bra under clothing is bad
  • Instagram filters makes things dificult

There are many more points to consider, but these are the important ones.

First thing you want to do is find a suitable pic that you want to x-ray.

Then go in to "Quick Select", and select the area you want to x-ray.
